Meet the instructor

Selam! Inkwuan dehna metachu. Mussie iballalo. Astemari negn. Beu hule hiwotye, amarignya menager alchilim. Gin, keu kidem.. yeteshale negn. Ahun.. megbat, manbeub, ena menager ichillalo. Ihko still fluent aydelehm. Meknatum amarignya betam meumar qebad kno. In g'hidy, I decided to make this a course. I think, for some of us.. (lijowoch yeu diaspora) the teaching of kwuan kwua achen got lost in the transition so please join me and follow along. Roots iniwsedalleun!
